Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a 3D print take?

The time required for a 3D print depends on the complexity and size of the design. Small objects can take a few hours, while larger or intricate models may take a day or more.

As of right now, we only provide PLA, but if you want ABS or a different type of filament, we would be happy to get it for you.

Absolutely! You can upload your design files in STL, OBJ, or other compatible formats. If you need assistance refining your design, we can help.
